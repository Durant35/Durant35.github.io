Detection, Classification and Tracking of Moving Objects in a 3D Environment

Azim A, Aycard O. Detection, classification and tracking of moving objects in a 3D environment[C]//Intelligent Vehicles Symposium (IV), 2012 IEEE. IEEE, 2012: 802-807.

Abstract

  • The basic idea…（基本思路）
    • use an octree based Occupancy Grid representation（基于八叉树的占有网格表示） to model dynamic environment surrounding the vehicle
    • detect moving objects based on inconsistencies between scans（帧与帧之间点云数据的区别/不一致）
  • …is the main contribution of this paper（主要贡献）
    • discrimination（区分） between moving and stationary objects without a priori knowledge of the targets（无需物体先验知识）
    • the detected moving objects are classified and tracked using Global Nearest Neighbor (GNN) technique（基于GNN，全局最近邻方法的动态物体分类与追踪）
  • The proposed method can be used in conjunction with（使用范围：适合处理所有激光雷达设备） any type of range sensors however we have demonstrated it using the data acquired from a Velodyne HDL-64E LIDAR sensor.
  • The merit of…（本文做法的优点）
    • efficient three dimensional representation of a dynamic environment（高效）
    • keeping in view the enormous amount of information provided by 3D range sensors（大数据量点云数据保持）

Introduction

  • 无人驾驶中感知部分的 2 subtasks
    • SLAM(Simultaneous Localization And Mapping) provides the vehicle with a map consisting of static entities of the environment
    • DATMO(Detection And Tracking of Moving Objects) uses that map to detect and track dynamic entities
  • 2D $\Longrightarrow$ 2.5D $\Longrightarrow$ 3D
    • 2D: These sensors scan the environment along a plane within a limited viewing angle thus the objects above or below this plane cannot be detected（只能扫描到一个平面）
    • 2.5D: Although there has been an overwhelming amount of work on perception in 2D and 2.5D
    • 3D: the problem of perception in 3D has been addressed by comparatively fewer researchers yet $\Longleftarrow$ 2 reasons
      • enormous amount of data provided by 3D sensors, several times larger than that of a 2D scan $\Longrightarrow$
        • ①：requires efficient algorithms and data structures
        • ②：the extraction and interpretation of geometry of 3D range data（点云数据几何信息的提取与解析） is very complicated in comparison to 2D
      • lower layers of the scanner usually perceive the ground or floor（感知到地面或者平面）which makes the interpretation of data a lot more complex
  • 目前的研究现状（侧重在 mapping）
    • Numbers of approaches are available in literature for solving SLAM and DATMO problem using 2D laser scanners
      • [One classical approach]use the grid maps：specially a well-established mapping technique of occupancy grid algorithm
    • 2D Mapping $\Longrightarrow$ Mapping of 3D outdoor environments
      • Mapping, a hard problem：① The noise in perception of the sensor；② the size of the environment to be mapped
      • 3D Mapping, even harder：the size of the grid increasing tremendously
    • Mapping of 3D outdoor environments
      • model the environment using rigid grid of cubic volumes of equal size (voxels)（体素） $\Longrightarrow$ not tractable in realtime and cannot be used for DATMO
      • [optimized solutions for outdoor environment mapping and detection of moving objects]: [11] R. Triebel, P. Pfaff, and W. Burgard. Multi-level surface maps for outdoor terrain mapping and loop closing. In International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems, 2006.
        • using multi-level surface maps
        • an efficient data structure using a 2D projection of the 3D space(reducing 3D space to a 2D map used for representing the environment and detecting motion) for outdoor mapping
        • $\Longrightarrow$ compromising（损失有用细节） the useful detail of 3D input space
      • [Another classical approach] perform mapping using point clouds to avoid the discretization of the environment（直接点云法避免环境离散化）
        • being a good representation
        • only provides information about the occupied areas and not the free or unknown areas therefore it cannot be used to perform detection and tracking of moving objects
      • An optimization of the 3D grid is possible by using a tree-based approach such as octrees
        • Octrees are used to partition a three dimensional space by recursively subdividing it into eight octants
        • Recently, has been used in several works for adapting occupancy grid mapping from 2D to 3D

          [3] J. Fournier, B. Ricard, and D. Laurendeau. Mapping and exploration of complex environments using persistent 3D model. In Proceedings of the Fourth Canadian Conference on Computer and Robot Vision,pages 403–410, Washington, DC, USA, 2007.
          [8] K. Pathak, A. Birk, and S. Schwertfeger. 3D forward sensor modeling and application to occupancy grid based sensor fusion. In International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems, 2007.
          [15] K. M. Wurm, A. Hornung, M. Bennewitz, C. Stachniss, and W. Burgard. OctoMap: A probabilistic, flexible, and compact 3D map representation for robotic systems. In Proceedings of the ICRA 2010 Workshop on Best Practice in 3D Perception and Modeling for Mobile Manipulation, Anchorage, AK, USA, May 2010.

        • $\Longrightarrow$ This paper used the octree-based 3D mapping approach described in [15] for mapping of the environment.
  • 本文的主要工作初次概述：presents a framework based on 3D range data to solve the problem of SLAM and DATMO in dynamic environments，引出下文
    • The basic idea is to use an octree based Occupancy Grid representation to model the dynamic environment surrounding the vehicle and to detect moving objects based on inconsistencies between scans.
    • The major contribution of this work is
      • the discrimination between moving and stationary objects without a priori knowledge of the targets.
      • detected moving objects are classified and tracked using Global Nearest Neighbor (GNN) technique.

3D-Mapping of the Environment, using SLAM

  • reliabe and efficient DATMO $\Longleftarrow$ good and efficient 3D mapping of the environment $\Longleftarrow$ octree based 3D occupancy grid approach presented in [15]
    • represent full 3D maps in which free and unknown areas are explicitly modeled
    • provides maximum flexibility regarding the area and resolution of the map（为地图面积和分辨率提供最大的灵活性）
  • localization of the vehicle is performed by an integrated navigation system (INS): ① GPS；② wheel speed sensors；③ inertial measurement unit (IMU) $\Longrightarrow$ provides the 6D pose, denoting the rear axis of the vehicle（车辆后轴为正前方）$\Longrightarrow$ used for calculating the tranformations between the scans（帧与帧点云数据间的位姿变换）
    • 改进（在 Future Work 中提到）：通过点云匹配如 ICP() 进行位姿估算 $\Longrightarrow$ 这里还可以与上面得到的 6D pose 进行融合
  • Octrees（八叉树） are used to partition a three dimensional space by recursively subdividing it into eight octants or cubic volumes (called voxels)
    • hierarchical data structure：the tree can be cut at any level to obtain a coarser subdivision
    • model occupancy of a volume based on the sensor measurements.
      • If a certain volume is measured as occupied, the corresponding node in the octree is initialized
      • Any uninitialized node could possibly be free or unknown in this boolean setting.
        • To resolve this ambiguity, free voxels are explicitly represented as free nodes in the tree.
  • occupancy is estimated probabilistically（基于概率的网格状态评估） which copes with the sensor noise（兼顾噪声）
    • The probability $P (n|z_{1:t})$ of a leaf node n being occupied given the sensor measurements $z_{1:t}$ is estimated
      $$ P (n|z_{1:t}) = [1 + \frac{1 - P (n|z_t)}{P (n|z_t)} \frac{1 - P (n|z_{1:t-1})}{P (n|z_{1:t-1})} \frac{P(n)}{1 - P(n)}]^{-1} $$
    • 基于 uniform prior distribution：$P(n) = 0.5$（均匀先验分布）的公式简化：
      $$ L(n|z_{1:t}) = L(n|z_{1:t-1}) + L(n|z_{t}),\quad where \space L(n|z) = log(P (n|z)/(1 − P (n|z)))$$
      • $L(n|z)$：logOdds(L) notation
      • $L(n|z_t)$ is the inverse sensor model which is specific to the sensor used for mapping $\Longrightarrow$ laser sensor (a beam-based inverse sensor model)：A ray-tracing operation（射线跟踪操作） is performed to efficiently calculate the volumes to be updated：
        $$L(n|z_{1:t}) = L(n|z_{1:t-1}) + L(n|z_{t}), \quad where \space L(n|z_{t}) = \begin{cases} l_{occupied}, if \space ray \space is \space reflected \space within \space volume \cr \space \space l_{free} \space \space , if \space ray \space traversed（穿过） volume \end{cases}$$
      • allows for faster updates in case of precomputed sensor models
    • clamping update policy（钳制更新策略，即上下限保证） proposed in [16]M. Yguel, O. Aycard, and C. Laugier. Update policy of dense maps: Efficient algorithms and sparse representation. In Field and Service Robotics, Results of the 6th International Conference, volume 42, pages 23–33, 2007. is used：
      $$L(n|z_{1:t}) = max(min(L(n|z_{1:t-1}) + L(n|z_t), l_{max}), l_{min})$$
      • Whenever the logOdds(L) value of a node reaches either the threshold $l_{min}$ or $l_{max}$, it is considered to be stable, and thus measured free or occupied with high confidence.
    • If all children of a node are stable leafs with the same occupancy state, then they can be pruned（树裁剪） leading to a considerable reduction in the total number of nodes.

Detection, Classfication and Tracking of Moving Objects

Detection of Moving Objects
  • 3D Mapping $\Longrightarrow$ consistent local map（连续局部地图） of the environment $\Longrightarrow$ based on the inconsistencies between observed free space and occupied space in the local grid map
    • borrows idea from background-subtraction methods in computer vision
    • process for the detection of moving objects is carried out in two steps:
      • detection of dynamic voxels（动态体素检测）
      • segmentation into individual dynamic objects（分离出单个动态对象）
  • Step-1：detect the voxels that might be containing measurements obtained from dynamic objects（检测可能属于动态障碍物的网格）
    • first construct a 3D occupancy grid map incrementally from laser measurements
    • inconsistencies between observed free space and occupied space in the local grid map $\Longrightarrow$ make a hypothesis about the voxels of the grid occupied by moving objects when new measurements arrive

      $S_{t-1}$：the state of a voxel in previous scan
      $S_t$： the state of a voxel in current scan
      第一种：$S_{t-1} = free$

      • ① $S_{t} = free$：无需处理
      • ② $S_{t} = occupied$：an object is detected on a location previously seen as free space $\Longrightarrow$ possibly a moving object，将该 voxel 添加到 dynamic voxels list 中

      第二种：$S_{t-1} = occupied$

      • ① $S_{t} = free$：a missed detection by the sensor； a voxel occupied by a dynamic object which may have displaced now $\Longrightarrow$ 对应 voxel 在 dynamic voxels list 中，假如 $S_{t+1} = free$，将其移除出 dynamic voxels list
      • ② $S_{t} = occupied$：probably is static；at a location which was previously unobserved→not clear（无法判断）$\Longrightarrow$ suppose that they are static until latter evidences come

      Result：all the inconsistencies between previous and current scans are identified as dynamic voxels

    • maintain a list of the voxles whose states are inconsistent between the current and previous scan $\Longrightarrow$ the list of all possible dynamic voxels
      • include a large number of sparsely situated voxels（稀疏体素） generated as a noise（噪点）
  • Step-2：the segmentation of detected dynamic measurements into regions（筛选动态网格组成物体集群）：clustering these dynamic voxels into seperate groups where each group represents a single object
    • clustering algorithm starts with stepping through dynamic voxels list（遍历上一步得到的列表）
      • 列表元素：A voxel is defined by the position of its center and the length of its side

        current voxel not yet assigned to any cluster：

        • ① a new cluster is initialized
        • ② find the set Neighbor(v) of its neighboring voxels from the list：Euclidean distance(the center of the current voxel, the voxel in consideration)
    • density threshold on the cluster to check if it has sufficient number of voxels to be identified as a moving object
      • 基于上一步聚集的 cluster，根据 cluster 密度信息

        ＜ threshold

        • ignored the cluster
        • voxels in cluster are removed from dynamic voxels list
      • get rid of the false alarms（错误检测） and spurious elements（稀疏元素） in the environment which were wrongly identified as dynamic object voxels
      • reduces the number of detected voxels by a large amount
      • remaining dynamic voxels in the list have a higher probabliity of corresponding to moving objects $\Longrightarrow$ further improved by classification
  • Detection 结果分析
    • ①：当前周边环境下的点云数据&incrementally constructed local static map from these point clouds（不断扩展的当前周边环境下的局部地图）
    • ②：根据前后帧所在局部地图 inconsistencies（不一致）得到的动态物体体素
      • sparse red points（稀疏红点）：噪声点
    • ③：集群规模阈值能够过滤掉大部分噪声点
      • Applying an appropriate threshold has eliminated quite a large number of wrongly detected dynamic voxels（集群内体素数量阈值过滤）
      • remain some clusters which do no belong to dynamic objects（还存在残余集群需通过分类和追踪过滤）
  • After detection of moving objects, the next step is to track them in order to estimate their states and predict their behaviors in the future.
    • simple object classification technique based on bounding box（基于边框）
  • Classification of the dynamic objects has also proved to play a great role in improving the results of tracking.
    • object tracking scheme using Global Nearest Neighborhood (GNN) data association and Kalman filter to track detected objects in 3D（先关联得到动态物体状态再过滤去除噪声）
Classification of Moving Objects
  • The classification of moving objects (specifically pedestrians, bicycles, vehicles etc) is of utmost importance（至关重要） for an intelligent vehicle $\Longrightarrow$ A variety of approaches have been developed in this field through supervised classification methods（监督分类方法） $\Longrightarrow$ require a sufficiently large number of training examples to ensure good performance in the test phase（测试阶段）
  • identify and track a cluster of dynamic cells provided by the previous step and fix a 3D bounding box（边框） to it
    • bounding box is calculated for connected components of moving objects in 3D space
    • classification is done based on the properties of these boxes
      • the ratio between their length, width and height（长/宽/高比例系数）
    • benefits：① highly time efficient；② works well for small numbers of moving objects（少量的移动物体）
    • shortcoming：the problem of occlusion cannot be solved（边框的封闭局限性）
      • Grouped regions for spatially close objects will form a combined blob（空间密切的物体所在体素容易聚成噪斑点（合成一个未知类的大物体））
      • $\Longrightarrow$ wrongly identified & cause tracking errors
  • Classification 结果分析
    • ①：Detection 得到的 clusters $\Longrightarrow$ calculate the bounding box corresponding to a dynamic object
    • ②：扣上边框的初步分类(car, bus/truck, bicycle/motorbike and pedestrian)，存在4个未定义类的小边框 $\Longleftarrow$ properties of each bounding box(ratio between length, width and height of the bounding box as an indicator)
    • ③：移除未定义类的边框，留下 car 、bike/motorbike 两类各一个物体
Tracking of Moving Objects
  • The tracking of multiple moving objects is a complex problem which is generally divided into two parts
    • filtering：the sequential estimation of the state of a dynamic object $\Longleftarrow$ predicting the positions of existing tracks（预测新位置）
      • using Bayesian filters $\Longleftarrow$ a specific motion model for tracked objects
    • data association：assign the observations to the existing tracks（将当前检测动态物体及其分类与已知追踪关联）$\Longrightarrow$ 准确的动态物体检测情况、分类类别与状态信息
      • Step-1：perform gating to find the likely observations to be associated with the existing tracks
        • 当前帧检测到的动态障碍物，与预测位置在最近邻域内的已知追踪关联
        • Global Nearest Neighbor (GNN) with classification information：finding possible nearnest neighbor association between a track and current observed moving objects, we ignore all the associations which consist of objects from different classes（不同类的 GNN 搜索可以直接跳过）
      • Step-2：track creation & deletion
        • track creation mechanism：the observations that are not associated to any of the existing tracks can possibly be the potential candidates for new tracks or the false alarms

          ① create a new track for each of these unused observations but those tracks are not confirmed yet（刚创建均为未确定追踪）

          • if：get associated to the observations for next consecutive scans $\Longrightarrow$ marked as confirmed object tracks（更新为确定追踪）
          • else: assumed to be false alarms and are suppressed subsequently（不再进行追踪）
        • track deletion mechanism

          ① we find no observation associated to a track, we mark it as not observed（标记为无关联追踪）
          ② deals with the cases when the object is temporarily occluded（物体临时被挡）

          • 重新预测无关联追踪新位置：predict the position of the object from its previous observations. 预测下一帧无关联追踪关联物体应该出现的位置
            ③ the object is not observed for next consecutive scans $\Longrightarrow$ assume that has moved out of the scanning area $\Longrightarrow$ track is deleted（连续两帧找不到关联的追踪）

Experimental Results

  • 测试数据来源的激光雷达设备（Velodyne HDL-64 High Definition Lidar）介绍
    • consists of a column of 64 single lasers
    • covering a pitch range（垂直角度范围） of approximately 26 degrees
    • rotates at a rate of 10 Hz
    • sweeping the complete horizontal ground plane and producing 180000 points per turn
  • There is no ground truth information（基准信息） available yet, therefore we have performed a qualitative evaluation（定性评估） of the performance.
    • ①：一帧点云数据
    • ②：当前帧对应的 occupancy grid（灰色） 以及基于该网格图提取的动态信息（红色）
    • ③：通过 Detection 得到的动态物体
    • ④：使用集群以及单 cluster 内 voxels 阈值过滤得到的动态物体信息
    • ⑤：初步 classification 的结果
    • ⑥：带 tracking 的分类结果
  • 实验结果小结
    • moving objects are clearly and efficiently segmented from the environment despite a considerable amount of noise.
    • The spurious elements（杂散元素） are further minimized by use of classification and tracking.
